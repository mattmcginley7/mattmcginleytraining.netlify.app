FAQ

Why Katch-McArdle?

It bases BMR on lean body mass, which makes it especially useful for lifters and people focused on body composition.

What if my body-fat estimate is not perfect?

That is normal. Use the estimate as a starting point, track your 7-day average body weight, and adjust calories by 5–10% if progress stalls for 2–3 weeks.

What macro ranges do you recommend?

Protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg, higher when cutting or very lean; fat around 0.6–1.0 g/kg; carbs fill the remaining calories.
